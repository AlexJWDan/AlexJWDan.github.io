Interests
Art, Drawing and Design

Drawing was my first introduction to the creative world. Every once in a while I would sit down, pick up a pencil and start drawing. Sometimes I would try to replicate the work and styles of my favourite artists other times I would just sketch the first thing that came to my mind. I was often driven by a passion to create the most aesthetic looking work I could.

During the Covid lockdown I discovered Adobe Illustrator after Adobe announced a free at-home Creative Cloud service for students impacted by the outbreak. As a result I began to try creating logos using the newly discovered software. However, my time with Illustrator was frustratingly cut short after the free subscription was terminated.
